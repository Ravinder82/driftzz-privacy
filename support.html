How can we help?

Welcome to the official support page for Driftzz: 3D Breathing & Calm. We are dedicated to providing a smooth, bug-free experience for your mindfulness journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • No Sound? Please check that your device's "Silent Mode" switch is off. The immersive soundscapes require system volume to be active.
  • Visuals Laggings? Driftzz is optimized for high performance. Ensure "Low Power Mode" is disabled on your device for the smoothest 60FPS experience.
  • Mood History Missing? Your data is stored locally on your device for privacy. If you delete the app, your history will be reset.
