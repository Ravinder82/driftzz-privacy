Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Driftzz collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app. This policy applies to all app features, including subscriptions.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and profile details provided via Google, Apple, or Email authentication.
  • Wellness Data: Information you log within the app, including mood check-ins, breathing session cycles, hydration levels, and tea pairings.
  • Gamification Data: Progress metrics such as XP (Experience Points), levels, streaks, and achievements.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with app features and immersive soundscapes.
  • Device Information: Diagnostic data to improve reliability and technical performance.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and personalize your wellness experience.
  • To track your progress and calculate growth metrics (XP and Streaks).
  • To provide precision recommendations based on your unique mood history.
  • To communicate important service-related updates and support.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

Driftzz subscriptions are processed exclusively by Apple via In-App Purchase. We do not collect or store your payment card details. Billing, cancellations, and refunds are managed directly by Apple.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • We use industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards (provided via Firebase/Google Cloud).
  • Biometrics: If you use Face ID or Touch ID to secure the app, that data is handled locally by your device's Secure Enclave and is never accessible to us or stored on our servers.
  • No system is perfectly secure, but we continuously work to protect your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited information only to operate the app:

  • With trusted infrastructure providers (Google Firebase/Apple Service).
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect safety and security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with app services.

7. Your Rights and Account Deletion

  • Request access to or correction of your data.
  • Delete Your Account: You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data directly through the app settings or by contacting us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Driftzz is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. International Users

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable protections.
